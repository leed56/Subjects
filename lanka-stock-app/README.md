# Lanka Stock & Accounts

A planning space for a **simple accounting + stock handling app** aimed at Sri Lankan small and medium businesses — grocery shops, electronics stores, electrical suppliers, and spare parts dealers.

## What this folder is

This is **not the app yet**. It holds product direction, feature priorities, and market notes before we build.

| Document | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| [ADVICE.md](./ADVICE.md) | What to build, what attracts shop owners, Sri Lanka–specific must-haves |
| [MVP-SCOPE.md](./MVP-SCOPE.md) | Lean first version — what to ship vs what to defer |
| [CATEGORIES.md](./CATEGORIES.md) | How grocery, electronics, electricals, and spare parts differ in stock handling |

## Suggested tech direction (when you build)

Keep it simple for v1:

- **Web app** (phone + PC) — most owners check sales from mobile
- **Offline-friendly** billing — internet drops are common
- **LKR only**, Sri Lanka timezone, Sinhala / Tamil / English UI
- **Cloud backup** with local-first option for counters
